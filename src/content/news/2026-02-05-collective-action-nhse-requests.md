Following the BMA's ballot result, Manchester LMC has prepared the following guidance for member practices considering participation in collective action.

## What is Collective Action?

Collective action refers to a set of coordinated steps that GPs and practices can take within the terms of the GMS/PMS contract to highlight the impact of underfunding and unsustainable workload. It is distinct from industrial action and does not involve breach of contract.

## NHSE Data Requests

Several practices have contacted us regarding data requests from NHS England. Our advice is as follows:

- You are not obliged to respond to voluntary data requests outside your contractual obligations
- Core contract data reporting requirements remain in place
- Where a request is ambiguous, contact Manchester LMC before responding

## What Actions Are Available?

The BMA has published a menu of actions at various levels of impact. Manchester LMC recommends that practices:

1. Review the BMA's guidance materials carefully
2. Discuss options with their PCN clinical director
3. Contact Manchester LMC if they require individual advice

## Support Available

Manchester LMC is available to support practices navigating collective action. We can provide confidential advice on specific data requests, contractual queries, and communications with commissioners. Please contact us via the office or use the secure form on our website.
