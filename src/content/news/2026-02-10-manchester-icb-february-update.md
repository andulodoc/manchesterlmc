The February 2026 meeting of Manchester Integrated Care Board covered several areas of significant interest to general practice across the city.

## Integrated Neighbourhood Teams

The ICB confirmed that all six Manchester localities are now operating Integrated Neighbourhood Teams (INTs) in some form, though the board acknowledged variation in how far integration with primary care has progressed. A review of INT governance structures is planned for Q1 2026/27.

## ARRS Funding Allocations

Updated guidance on Additional Roles Reimbursement Scheme allocations was circulated ahead of the new financial year. PCNs are reminded that unused ARRS funding from 2025/26 cannot be carried forward. Manchester LMC will be issuing separate guidance on workforce planning implications.

## CQC Inspection Updates

Following recent inspections of Manchester practices, the ICB outlined plans for a dedicated support programme for practices rated Requires Improvement. Manchester LMC continues to advise practices receiving inspection notices and can provide representation support — contact the office for details.

## Next Steps

The next ICB board meeting is scheduled for March 2026. Manchester LMC will continue to attend as a standing observer and will report back on any items of relevance to member practices.
