The BMA's General Practitioners Committee has reached agreement on the updated GP contract for 2026, bringing several significant changes that will affect practices across Manchester.

## Key Changes

### Enhanced Access Funding

Practices will see an increase in Enhanced Access funding, allowing greater flexibility in how additional appointment capacity is delivered. The requirement for specific session times has been relaxed.

### Investment and Impact Fund

The IIF has been simplified from 36 indicators to 18, reducing administrative burden while maintaining focus on areas of greatest patient benefit.

### Premises Costs

Longstanding issues around premises cost inflation have been partially addressed, with a new formula linking reimbursement more closely to actual market rates.

## What This Means for Manchester Practices

Manchester LMC has been actively engaged in the negotiation process and welcomes these changes as a step in the right direction. We will be running a webinar for member practices in March to discuss the implications in detail.
