GP Contract 2026/27: Manchester LMC Briefing

Following the BMA GPC's provisional agreement on the 2026/27 GP contract, Manchester LMC has prepared a comprehensive briefing setting out the key changes to funding, workload targets, and contractual obligations for practices.
