Post a Vacancy

Listing a role on the Manchester LMC job board is free for member practices and £50 for non-members. Listings remain live for 4 weeks or until you request removal.

Members Only

You must be logged in as a Manchester LMC subscriber to post a vacancy. If you are not yet a member, you can register for a free practice membership or a £100/year locum subscription.
