What We Can Do For You

Confidential, expert support for GPs and practice staff across Manchester — whatever the challenge, we're in your corner.

About our support services

Manchester LMC is the statutory representative and advisory body for approximately 360 GPs across 90 practices in Manchester. Our experienced team provides free, confidential advice on the full range of issues facing general practice — from complex regulatory matters to everyday employment and contractual questions. We do not judge; we listen and we help.

Whether you are facing a GMC investigation, a difficult partnership situation, a CQC visit, or simply need to talk something through, please do not hesitate to contact us. All enquiries are treated in strict confidence.

All calls and enquiries are treated in strict confidence. Manchester LMC will never share your details with your employer, the ICB, NHS England, or any other body without your explicit consent.

Confidential Advisory Service

Facing a GMC investigation, fitness to practise referral, complaint, or suspension? Involved in a CQC inspection or partnership dispute? Our experienced advisors provide immediate, confidential guidance — free of charge and without judgement.

  • GMC investigations & fitness to practise
  • Patient complaints & NHS ombudsman
  • Suspension & immediate action cases
  • CQC inspections & enforcement
  • Partnership disputes & practice difficulties

Wellbeing Hub

GP wellbeing is central to safe, sustainable patient care. Our Wellbeing Hub brings together mental health support, peer networks, free apps, crisis lines, and financial assistance resources — all in one place, specifically for healthcare professionals in Manchester.

  • NHS Practitioner Health & BMA counselling
  • Peer support & mentoring schemes
  • Free apps: Headspace, SilverCloud, Unmind
  • 24/7 crisis support lines

Practice Support

Practical, expert guidance on the operational and legal challenges that practices face every day. We help you navigate employment law, HR matters, staff disciplinary issues, contracts, and policy implementation — saving you time and reducing risk.

  • Employment matters & HR guidance
  • Staff disciplinary & grievance processes
  • GMS/PMS contract interpretation
  • Policies, procedures & templates

Breach Reporting

If your ICB, NHS England, or a local trust is failing to meet its contractual obligations to your practice, you have the right to formally report this. We will advise you on the process and support you throughout any escalation.

  • ICB contractual breaches
  • Trust & secondary care failures
  • Formal dispute escalation support

Cameron Fund

The Cameron Fund provides financial assistance to doctors and their families who are experiencing financial difficulty — whether due to illness, mental health difficulties, family crisis, or other hardship. Grants are means-tested and entirely confidential.

The Fund has supported doctors in need since 1970. Applications are straightforward and the team is supportive and discreet throughout the process.

Cameron Fund at a glance

  • Open to all registered GPs
  • Grants and interest-free loans
  • Strictly confidential process
  • No obligation application
