What's in the Members Area

Everything you need to stay informed and connected as a Manchester GP — in one secure place.

Agenda & Minutes

Download agenda papers before meetings and read minutes afterwards. Stay informed on decisions that affect your practice.

Open Forum

A private, members-only discussion space. Raise issues, share experiences, and connect with GPs from across Manchester.

Subscriber Briefings

In-depth member briefings on contracts, policy changes, and local GP news — separate from the public newsletter.

Confidential Advice

Access to confidential advisory support from our Managing Director — for GMC, CQC, partnership, or contractual concerns.

Post Vacancies

Member practices can post GP and practice staff vacancies directly to the Manchester LMC job board — free of charge.

Conference Representation

Member GPs are eligible to stand as LMC conference representatives, taking Manchester's voice to national BMA/GPC meetings.

Membership FAQs

GP partners, salaried GPs, and practice staff at NHS-contracted Manchester practices are covered by the practice levy and do not need to pay separately. Contact us at info@manchesterlmc.co.uk to set up your account and we will verify your practice membership.

Use the "Forgotten your password?" link on the login form. You will receive a reset link to your registered email address. If you no longer have access to that email address, contact us directly and we will update your details.

After you submit the registration form, you will receive an email with a secure payment link. The £100 annual subscription is payable by card. Your account will be activated within one working day of payment being confirmed. You will receive a reminder when your subscription is due for renewal.

Yes. Subscriptions are annual and non-refundable once paid, but you can choose not to renew at the end of your subscription year. Simply do not respond to the renewal notice and your account access will lapse automatically. To cancel early due to exceptional circumstances, contact us.
